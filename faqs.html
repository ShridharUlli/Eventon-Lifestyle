FAQs

Q. How and where do I collect my tickets/wristbands?

A. From the box office at the event venue, in exchange for your e-ticket copy. Wristbands will be given at the entry gates.

Q. What is an Early Bird GA/VIP Ticket?

A. Early Bird GA/VIP tickets are limited general access/VIP access tickets available at a discounted rate giving you access into the GA/VIP zone at the Venue

Q. Can I buy tickets using cash?

A. You can buy tickets using cash through our Official Offline Promoters

Q. How old do I have to be to enter PiratesNite 2020?

A. You must be of 15 years and above to enter the event. Below ages should be accompanied by an adult.

Q. Will I be able to get a refund for the tickets if I do not attend the party?

A. No refund will be provided for tickets even in case of any rescheduling.

Q. What should I do if I purchase a ticket, but live outside India?

A. Please provide a copy of your passport,VISA copy and credit card/debit card at the ticketing booth at the venue for your tickets.

Q. Where can I read ticketing `Terms and Conditions`?

A. The ticket terms and conditions will be provided before you purchase your ticket.

Q. Can I re-enter the venue during the day if I leave?

A. No, unfortunately, no re-entry is allowed for the event. Your wristband will be cut upon exit. Exception for medical emergencies.
